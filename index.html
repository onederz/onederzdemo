Stories From Happy Travelers

KN

Kyra nope

Best hostel ever. The people were amazing, loads of activities organized by the hostel so we got to know loads of people. We had a private room and they changed the sheets and fresh towels every day. Food in the hostel was good and staff very kind. Would almost go back to Siem Reap just for the hostel.

KC

Klaudia C

This is literally the best hostel in SEA. The staff kindness is on another level. 🥹 The pools are amazing. Towel change whenever you need. Hostel bar & restaurant, so you dont have to come out of hostel if you dont want to. Even the coffee is great. And the vibes!!!

WN

Winnie Nkosi

I loved it. Didn’t spend much time at the hostel, didn’t join any of the activities they plan, next time I’m there I definitely will. I usually book hostels because I only go there to sleep, but this one you can chill and enjoy as it’s super clean and lots to do. I did enjoy breakfast on one of the days. I recommend❤️
