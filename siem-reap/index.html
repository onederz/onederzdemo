Finding Your Way to Onederz

From Siem Reap Angkor International Airport (SIA)

By Tuk-Tuk: The easiest option. The official airport tuk-tuk stand is outside arrivals. The fare is approximately $12–$15 USD. The journey takes about 45 minutes to 1 hour.

By Taxi: Available at the airport. A comfortable option for larger groups or those with lots of luggage. Fare is approximately $25–$30 USD.

By Bus: A shuttle bus service runs between the airport and Siem Reap town. Tickets cost around $8 USD per person. The journey takes about 1 hour and drops passengers at the official bus stop on the edge of town. From there, you’ll need a short tuk-tuk ride (about $2–$3 USD) to reach Pub Street or Onederz Hostel.

From the Bus Station

By Tuk-Tuk: Most bus companies arrive at stations on the edge of town (such as Giant Ibis, Virak Buntham, or Nattakan). From there, it’s a short tuk-tuk ride to the city center. Simply tell the driver "Onederz Siem Reap, near Pub Street." The fare is usually $3–$5 USD, depending on distance.

Address for Drivers

Onederz Siem Reap

Pro Tip: Simply tell your tuk-tuk driver "Onederz, near Pub Street" – they will know exactly where to go!
